WHAT IS A PORTDURNFORD OVC CENTRE?

OVC is short for Orphans and Vulnerable Children, therefore a Portdurnford OVC Centre is a place that provides a range support proframs for children who are Orphans and vulnerable children and vulnerable community who needs support, care and love. It is a place where OVC children and vulnerable community victims of GBV, Unemployed youth are assisted with educational, emotional, social support, including identifying and developing their individual talents developing skills and preventing stigma to the community. Our vision is to build up Vulnerable communities, encouraging children to love education and empowering them to choose career that suite their goals, to be an effective organization that will reduce stigma and discrimination, GBVF, Drugs Abuse, teenage pregnancy, sexual transmitted disease, HIV and AIDS, malnutrition and all social ills that can effect our community. Our mission is to giving Vulnerable community a safe space and a place where they can refer their issues without being judged providing OVCs well balanced healthy food, we give hope teach them good communication skills working with other organization to provide healthy life style in our community.
